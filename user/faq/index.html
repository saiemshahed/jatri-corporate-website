The app provides tracking and ticketing services. Once you enter your location and destination, the app shows the current location of all the buses on your route.

Previously, users could recharge their accounts from our recharge points in each bus stop. Jatri now supports digital payment, so you can pay for your subscription or bus fare directly from your digital accounts!
